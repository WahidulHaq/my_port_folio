This folder should contain the following image files:

1. hero-bg.jpg - A high-quality background image for the hero section (1920x1080px)
2. profile.jpg - Your profile photo for the About section (600x600px)
3. portfolio-1.jpg to portfolio-6.jpg - Images for your portfolio projects (800x600px)
4. testimonial-1.jpg to testimonial-3.jpg - Profile photos for testimonials (200x200px)

You can replace these with your own images or use stock photos from free sites like:
- Unsplash (https://unsplash.com)
- Pexels (https://pexels.com)
- Pixabay (https://pixabay.com)

Make sure to optimize your images for web to keep loading times fast.
